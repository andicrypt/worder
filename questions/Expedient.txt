Question:
In the context of moral decision-making, which of the following scenarios best exemplifies the use of an expedient solution?

A) Choosing to confront a difficult situation head-on, regardless of the potential consequences.
B) Deciding to take a shortcut that violates company policy in order to meet a tight deadline.
C) Seeking advice from a mentor to navigate a complex ethical dilemma with integrity.
D) Investing time and effort in building long-lasting relationships based on trust and respect.

Correct Answer:
B) Deciding to take a shortcut that violates company policy in order to meet a tight deadline.
