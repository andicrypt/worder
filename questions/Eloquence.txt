**Question:**

Which of the following best describes the quality of "eloquence"?

A) The ability to use language in a way that impresses or persuades others.  
B) The skill of quickly solving mathematical problems.  
C) The talent for creating intricate artwork.  
D) The capacity to empathize deeply with others.  

**Correct Answer: A) The ability to use language in a way that impresses or persuades others.**
