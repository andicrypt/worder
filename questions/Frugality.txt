**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best illustrates the concept of "frugality"?

A) Jenna spent her entire paycheck on a designer handbag, leaving her with nothing for the rest of the month.

B) Mark meticulously planned his budget to ensure he could save a portion of his income each month for future emergencies.

C) Karen impulsively purchased an extravagant vacation package without considering her financial situation.

D) Tom always insists on ordering the most expensive item on the menu whenever he dines out.

**Correct Answer:**

B) Mark meticulously planned his budget to ensure he could save a portion of his income each month for future emergencies.
