**Question:**

In the context of evaluating the impact of climate change policies, which of the following statements best describes the "bearing" of economic factors?

A) Economic factors have no influence on the outcome of climate change policies.

B) Economic factors are crucial as they provide the necessary funding for climate change initiatives.

C) Economic factors have a strong bearing on climate change policies as they determine the feasibility and sustainability of such policies.

D) Economic factors should be disregarded because they complicate the implementation of climate change policies.

**Correct Answer:**

C) Economic factors have a strong bearing on climate change policies as they determine the feasibility and sustainability of such policies.
