**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios can best be described as "quixotic"?

A) A scientist meticulously designs an experiment to test a new hypothesis.

B) A young artist dreams of creating a masterpiece that will unite all countries in peace and harmony.

C) A teacher develops a comprehensive syllabus for an upcoming academic year.

D) A chef carefully follows a recipe to bake a cake for a party.

**Answer:**

B) A young artist dreams of creating a masterpiece that will unite all countries in peace and harmony.
