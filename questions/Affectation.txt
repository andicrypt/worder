Question:  
Which of the following sentences best illustrates the meaning of the word "affectation"?

A) The scientist explained the complex theory in simple terms to ensure everyone understood.  
B) The actor's public persona was an affectation, masking his true, more humble personality.  
C) The teacher encouraged her students to write with clarity and avoid unnecessary details.  
D) The athlete's rigorous training schedule was crucial to her performance success.  

Correct Answer: B) The actor's public persona was an affectation, masking his true, more humble personality.
