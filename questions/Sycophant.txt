**Question:**

Which of the following best describes a "sycophant"?

A) A person who criticizes authority figures to improve their own status.

B) A person who acts obsequiously towards someone important in order to gain advantage.

C) A person who remains indifferent to social hierarchies and power dynamics.

D) A person who consistently disagrees with those in power to challenge the status quo.

**Correct Answer: B) A person who acts obsequiously towards someone important in order to gain advantage.**
