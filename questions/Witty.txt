**Question:**

Which of the following sentences uses the word "witty" correctly?

A) The scientist's explanation of the complex theory was so witty that even those without a background in physics could understand it.

B) The comedian's jokes were so witty that the audience couldn't stop laughing throughout the entire performance.

C) The chef's new recipe was so witty that it won first place in the culinary competition.

D) The athlete's performance was incredibly witty, breaking several records in the process.

**Correct Answer:**

B) The comedian's jokes were so witty that the audience couldn't stop laughing throughout the entire performance.
