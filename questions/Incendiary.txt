**Question:**  

The politician's speech was criticized for its ____ nature, as it seemed designed to provoke anger and division among the audience.

A) *Conciliatory*  
B) *Incendiary*  
C) *Pedantic*  
D) *Laconic*  

**Correct Answer:** B) *Incendiary*  

**Explanation:**  
The word "incendiary" is used to describe something that is intended to stir up conflict or provoke strong emotions. In this context, the speech is described as having a nature that provokes anger and division, which aligns with the definition of "incendiary."
