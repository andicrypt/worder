**Question:**

Choose the word or phrase that is most similar in meaning to the word "interminable."

A) Fleeting  
B) Perpetual  
C) Concise  
D) Sporadic  

**Correct Answer: B) Perpetual**

**Question:**

The professor's lecture was so detailed and comprehensive that many students found it to be interminable. Which of the following words best describes the lecture?

A) Concise  
B) Tedious  
C) Sporadic  
D) Engaging  

**Correct Answer:** B) Tedious
