**Question:**

In the context of social interactions, someone who is described as "gawky" is most likely to be:

A) Graceful  
B) Confident  
C) Awkward  
D) Charismatic  

**Correct Answer:** C) Awkward

Question: 

In the context of describing a person's physical demeanor, which of the following words most closely matches the meaning of "gawky"?

A) Graceful  
B) Elegant  
C) Ungainly  
D) Poised  

Correct Answer: C) Ungainly
