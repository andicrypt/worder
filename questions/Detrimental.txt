**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates something that is *detrimental*?

A) A new policy that increases employee productivity and morale.

B) A diet that improves overall health and reduces the risk of diseases.

C) A pollutant that causes damage to the environment and harms wildlife. *(Correct Answer)*

D) A training program that enhances skills and boosts career opportunities.

**Answer:**

C) A pollutant that causes damage to the environment and harms wildlife. *(Correct Answer)*
