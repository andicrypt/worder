**Question:**  

Which of the following sentences best uses the word "pejorative"?  

A) The scientist's theory was met with pejorative acclaim, earning awards from various prestigious institutions.  
B) The author's pejorative remarks about the novel suggested that she had a deep appreciation for its themes.  
C) The coach's pejorative comments about the team's performance left the players feeling discouraged and undervalued.  
D) The pejorative painting was displayed prominently in the museum, celebrated for its vibrant colors and innovative techniques.  

**Correct Answer:** C) The coach's pejorative comments about the team's performance left the players feeling discouraged and undervalued.
