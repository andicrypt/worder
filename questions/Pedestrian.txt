Question: In the context of creative writing, which of the following sentences best illustrates the meaning of the word "pedestrian"?

A) The novel's complex characters and intricate plot twists kept readers on the edge of their seats.

B) The author's pedestrian storytelling failed to captivate the audience, leaving them uninterested in the outcome.

C) The vivid imagery and poetic language in the poem transported the readers to another world.

D) The groundbreaking research presented at the conference opened new avenues for scientific exploration.

Correct Answer: B) The author's pedestrian storytelling failed to captivate the audience, leaving them uninterested in the outcome.
