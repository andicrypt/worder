**Question:**  

Which of the following sentences uses the word "optimistic" correctly?

A) Despite the forecast of rain, Jenna remained optimistic that the outdoor concert would not be canceled.  
B) The chef was optimistic in his use of spice, creating a dish that was far too bland for everyone's taste.  
C) The pessimistic scientist was optimistic about the failure of her experiment.  
D) The cat was optimistic to climb the tree, but found itself stuck halfway up.

**Correct Answer:** A) Despite the forecast of rain, Jenna remained optimistic that the outdoor concert would not be canceled.
