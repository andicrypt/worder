Choose the word that most closely matches the definition of the word "Malleable."

A) Stubborn  
B) Rigid  
C) Pliable  
D) Inflexible  

Correct Answer: C) Pliable

**Question:**  
In the context of personality traits, which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "malleable"?

A) Stubborn  
B) Adaptable  
C) Rigid  
D) Inflexible  

**Correct Answer:** B) Adaptable
