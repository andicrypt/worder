Question: 

Which of the following sentences uses the word "devious" correctly?

A) The devious path through the park was perfect for a leisurely afternoon stroll, with its winding turns and scenic views.  
B) Her devious command of the violin left the audience in awe of her straightforward and predictable performance.  
C) He was known for his devious honesty, always speaking in the most direct and clear manner possible.  
D) The devious weather forecast promised a week of sunny skies, and indeed, it was exactly what followed.  

Correct Answer: A) The devious path through the park was perfect for a leisurely afternoon stroll, with its winding turns and scenic views.
