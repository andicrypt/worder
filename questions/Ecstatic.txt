**Question:**

Which of the following sentences uses the word "ecstatic" correctly?

A) The scientist remained ecstatic, carefully analyzing the data with meticulous precision.

B) After hearing the news of her promotion, Maria was ecstatic, unable to stop smiling and celebrating with her friends. 

C) The judge remained ecstatic as he impartially delivered the verdict after the long trial.

D) The artist's style was described as ecstatic, characterized by muted colors and subdued themes.

**Answer:**

B) After hearing the news of her promotion, Maria was ecstatic, unable to stop smiling and celebrating with her friends.
