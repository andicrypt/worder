Which of the following best describes a "skeptic"?

A) A person who readily accepts new ideas without question.  
B) A person who is inclined to question or doubt accepted opinions.  
C) A person who is known for their unwavering trust in authority.  
D) A person who is indifferent to new information or perspectives.  

**Correct Answer: B) A person who is inclined to question or doubt accepted opinions.**
