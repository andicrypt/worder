**Question:**  

Which of the following sentences uses the word "prudent" correctly?  

A) The artist's prudent brushstrokes were wild and chaotic, creating a sense of disorder on the canvas.  
B) Her prudent decision to save money each month allowed her to retire comfortably.  
C) His prudent behavior at the party was the cause of his outrageous dance moves.  
D) The chef's prudent use of spices resulted in a dish that was overwhelmingly hot and spicy.  

**Correct Answer:** B) Her prudent decision to save money each month allowed her to retire comfortably.
