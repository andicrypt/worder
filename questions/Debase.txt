Which of the following sentences uses the word "debase" correctly?

A) The artist's intention was to debase the sculpture by adding intricate details and vibrant colors.  
B) The company feared that the scandal would debase its reputation and lead to a loss of customers.  
C) The teacher aimed to debase the students' understanding by providing clear and concise explanations.  
D) The chef's goal was to debase the flavors by using the freshest ingredients available.  

**Correct Answer: B) The company feared that the scandal would debase its reputation and lead to a loss of customers.**
