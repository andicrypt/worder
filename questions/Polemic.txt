**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best illustrates the use of the word "polemic"?

A) The professor's lecture was a calm and balanced overview of the topic, avoiding any controversial opinions.

B) The author's latest book is a polemic, criticizing modern technology for its role in disrupting traditional communication.

C) The artist's new painting depicts a serene landscape, evoking feelings of peace and tranquility.

D) The students engaged in a productive discussion, each respecting the other's viewpoints.

**Answer:**

B) The author's latest book is a polemic, criticizing modern technology for its role in disrupting traditional communication.
