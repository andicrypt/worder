**Question:**  
The professor's lecture was so _______ that many students felt more like they were being scolded than educated.

**Options:**  
A) didactic  
B) whimsical  
C) benevolent  
D) apathetic  

**Correct Answer:**  
A) didactic

**Question:**

In his speech, the professor adopted a(n) _______ tone, which made the audience feel as though they were being lectured to rather than engaged in a conversation.

A) conciliatory  
B) didactic  
C) apathetic  
D) enigmatic  

**Correct Answer: B) didactic**
