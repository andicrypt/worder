**Question:**

In the context of organizational leadership, a manager described as hidebound would most likely be:

A) Open to innovative strategies and flexible in decision-making.

B) Resistant to adopting new methods and reliant on traditional approaches.

C) Enthusiastic about exploring uncharted territories and embracing change.

D) Indifferent to the outcomes and apathetic towards team development.

**Correct Answer:**

B) Resistant to adopting new methods and reliant on traditional approaches.
