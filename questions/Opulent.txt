Question:  
Which of the following sentences uses the word "opulent" correctly?

A) Despite his opulent appetite, he managed to maintain a slim figure.  
B) The opulent library was filled with rare books and lavish decorations.  
C) Her opulent personality made her the most approachable person in the room.  
D) The opulent weather made it impossible to predict the storm.  

Correct Answer:  
B) The opulent library was filled with rare books and lavish decorations.
