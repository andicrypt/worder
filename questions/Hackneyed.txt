**Question:**  

Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "hackneyed"?  

A) Innovative  
B) Original  
C) Trite  
D) Unique  

**Correct Answer:** C) Trite

**Question:**  

Which of the following sentences best illustrates the meaning of the word "hackneyed"?  

A) The author's new novel was filled with vibrant and original metaphors that captivated readers.  
B) The professor's lecture was full of hackneyed clichés that bored the students.  
C) The scientist proposed a groundbreaking theory that challenged conventional wisdom.  
D) The artist's work was celebrated for its innovative use of color and form.  

**Correct Answer:** B) The professor's lecture was full of hackneyed clichés that bored the students.
