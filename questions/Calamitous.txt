Choose the word or phrase that is most similar in meaning to "calamitous."

A) Fortuitous  
B) Beneficial  
C) Catastrophic  
D) Harmless  

Correct Answer: C) Catastrophic

**Question:**

In the aftermath of the hurricane, the residents found themselves grappling with the _____ consequences of the storm, as homes were destroyed and infrastructure was severely damaged.

A) Beneficial  
B) Calamitous  
C) Trivial  
D) Insignificant  

**Answer:**

B) Calamitous
