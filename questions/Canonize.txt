Question:
In the eyes of his followers, the artist was __________ for his revolutionary approach to painting that challenged traditional norms and styles.

A) vilified
B) canonized
C) disparaged
D) denigrated

Correct Answer: B) canonized

Question:
In medieval Europe, it was common for the Catholic Church to **canonize** individuals who were deemed to be exceptionally virtuous and worthy of veneration. In this context, "canonize" most closely means:

A) criticize  
B) excommunicate  
C) sanctify  
D) ostracize  

Correct Answer: C) sanctify
