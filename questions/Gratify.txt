**Question:**

In an effort to gratify her parents, Maria spent extra hours studying for her exams, hoping to achieve top grades.

Which of the following words is closest in meaning to "gratify" as used in the sentence above?

A) Annoy  
B) Satisfy  
C) Ignore  
D) Challenge  

**Correct Answer: B) Satisfy**

**Question:**

In an effort to _______ his guests, the host ensured that every detail of the dinner party was meticulously planned and executed.

A) aggravate  
B) gratify  
C) obscure  
D) deceive  

**Correct Answer: B) gratify**
