**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best uses the word "invigorate"?

A) The marathon runner felt invigorate after the long race, needing nothing more than rest.

B) The herbal tea was known to invigorate the drinker, providing a refreshing burst of energy.

C) She decided to invigorate her wardrobe by donating all her colorful clothes to charity.

D) The speech was meant to invigorate its audience, but instead, many found it confusing and dull.

**Correct Answer:** B) The herbal tea was known to invigorate the drinker, providing a refreshing burst of energy.
