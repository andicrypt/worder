**Question:**

In the crowded lecture hall, the professor's monotonous voice droned on, and it wasn't long before the students started to feel restive. Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "restive" as used in this sentence?

A) Serene  
B) Complacent  
C) Agitated  
D) Obedient  

**Correct Answer: C) Agitated**
