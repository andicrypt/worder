**Question:**

In the context of persuasive writing, a speaker's ______________ tone is likely intended to motivate the audience to take specific action through compelling arguments and passionate appeals.

A) indifferent  
B) hortatory  
C) apathetic  
D) dismissive  

**Correct Answer: B) hortatory**

**Question:**

In the context of a motivational speech, which of the following words best describes a speaker who aims to inspire the audience to take immediate action on climate change?

A) Hortatory  
B) Indifferent  
C) Lethargic  
D) Reticent  

**Answer:**

A) Hortatory
