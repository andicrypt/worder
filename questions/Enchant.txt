**Vocabulary Question:**

The word "enchant" most nearly means:

A) **Bewitch**

B) Repel

C) Disregard

D) Confuse

**Correct Answer:** A) Bewitch

Choose the word or phrase that most nearly means the same as the word "enchant."

A) Befuddle  
B) Captivate  
C) Disenchant  
D) Dispel  

Correct Answer: B) Captivate
