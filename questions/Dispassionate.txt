Question:
In order to make a fair and __________ decision, the judge remained dispassionate during the trial, carefully considering all the evidence presented.
A) emotional
B) impartial
C) biased
D) passionate

Correct answer: B) impartial

Question:
Which of the following best describes someone who is dispassionate?

A) Emotional
B) Biased
C) Impulsive
D) Calm

Correct answer: D) Calm
