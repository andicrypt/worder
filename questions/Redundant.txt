**Question:**

In the context of a written report, which of the following sentences best illustrates the concept of a "redundant" statement?

A) The report includes a detailed analysis of the data collected from various sources.

B) The report repeatedly mentions the same statistics in multiple sections.

C) The report concludes with a summary of the findings and recommendations.

D) The report begins with an introduction that outlines its objectives.

**Correct Answer:** B) The report repeatedly mentions the same statistics in multiple sections.
