Question: 

In her presentation about climate change, the scientist aimed to distill the vast amount of data into a format that was both understandable and impactful for her audience. Which of the following best describes what she was trying to achieve?

A) To complicate the information by adding unnecessary details

B) To extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of the information

C) To obscure the data by using technical jargon

D) To summarize the data inaccurately

Correct Answer: B) To extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of the information
