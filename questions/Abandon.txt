**Question:**

In the context of the artist's performance, the term "abandon" is best exemplified by which of the following scenarios?

A) The artist carefully followed every movement as choreographed, ensuring precision and accuracy in her dance steps.

B) The artist unleashed her creativity on stage, dancing with a wildness and freedom that captivated the audience. 

C) The artist paused frequently to consult her notes, ensuring that she adhered strictly to the planned script.

D) The artist displayed a calm and composed demeanor, maintaining complete control over her emotions throughout the performance.

**Correct Answer: B**
