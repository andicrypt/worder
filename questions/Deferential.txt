**Question:**

In the context of a workplace meeting, an employee who is described as "deferential" would most likely:

A) Assertively challenge the ideas of others to promote their own agenda.

B) Indifferently disregard the opinions of their colleagues.

C) Respectfully submit to the opinions and ideas of their supervisor. 

D) Critically analyze every suggestion before agreeing to it.

**Correct Answer:** C) Respectfully submit to the opinions and ideas of their supervisor.
