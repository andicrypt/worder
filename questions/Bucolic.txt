Question:  

Which of the following sentences uses the word "bucolic" correctly?

A) The bustling city center was known for its bucolic atmosphere, filled with skyscrapers and busy streets.  
B) She found the bucolic setting of the small village, with its rolling hills and grazing sheep, to be the perfect escape from city life.  
C) His bucolic demeanor made him the life of the party, always energetic and talkative.  
D) The bucolic storm caused widespread damage, uprooting trees and flooding roads.

Correct Answer:  
B) She found the bucolic setting of the small village, with its rolling hills and grazing sheep, to be the perfect escape from city life.
