**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best demonstrates the meaning of the word "daunt"?

A) The loud music at the festival made everyone feel excited and energetic.

B) The sheer size of the mountain did not daunt the experienced climbers, who were eager to reach the summit. 

C) The calm and serene environment of the library was perfect for studying without any distractions.

D) The glowing reviews of the restaurant enticed many food enthusiasts to try the new menu.

**Correct Answer:**

B) The sheer size of the mountain did not daunt the experienced climbers, who were eager to reach the summit.
