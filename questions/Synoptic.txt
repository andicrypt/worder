**Question:**  
The professor asked the students to provide a synoptic view of the research article. What did the professor want the students to provide?

**Options:**  
A) An in-depth analysis of the methodologies used in the research.  
B) A detailed critique of the weaknesses in the research article.  
C) A general summary or overview of the main points in the research article.  
D) A comparison of this research article with other similar studies.  

**Correct Answer:**  
C) A general summary or overview of the main points in the research article.
