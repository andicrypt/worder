**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best demonstrates the meaning of the word "mortify"?

A) After winning the championship, the athlete felt a deep sense of accomplishment and joy.

B) The teacher's praise for her essay made her feel proud and confident.

C) Tripping on stage during the performance was enough to mortify the actor in front of the entire audience.

D) The comedian's joke was so funny that it made everyone in the room laugh uncontrollably.

**Correct Answer: C) Tripping on stage during the performance was enough to mortify the actor in front of the entire audience.**
