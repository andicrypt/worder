**Question:**  

The novel's portrayal of the government as deceitful and oppressive serves as an example of subversion because it __________.

A) supports the established norms of society.  
B) reinforces the authority of the ruling party.  
C) undermines the power and authority of the government.  
D) praises the effectiveness of the current system.  

**Correct Answer: C) undermines the power and authority of the government.**
