Choose the word that is most similar in meaning to "humdrum."

A) Exhilarating  
B) Mundane  
C) Unpredictable  
D) Vibrant  

**Correct Answer: B) Mundane**  

Explanation: "Humdrum" means lacking variety; dull, monotonous, and commonplace. The word "mundane" similarly suggests something that is ordinary or lacking excitement, making it the closest synonym among the options.
