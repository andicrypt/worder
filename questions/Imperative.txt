**Question:**

In the context of environmental conservation, it is __________ that we reduce our carbon footprint to mitigate the effects of climate change.

A) Trivial  
B) Imperative  
C) Optional  
D) Negligible  

**Correct Answer:** B) Imperative

**Question:**

In the context of environmental conservation, addressing climate change has become an __________ issue for governments worldwide, requiring immediate and decisive action.

A) Optional  
B) Trivial  
C) Imperative  
D) Negligible  

**Correct Answer:** C) Imperative
