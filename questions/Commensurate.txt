**Question:**

In the context of salary negotiations, the term "commensurate" is often used to express the idea that compensation should be:

A) Limited and insufficient

B) Random and unpredictable

C) Opposed and contradictory

D) Corresponding and proportional

**Answer:**

D) Corresponding and proportional

**Vocabulary Question:**

In order to be fair, the compensation package offered to the new employee was made __________ with her level of experience and the responsibilities of the position.

A) incongruent  
B) commensurate  
C) negligible  
D) excessive  

**Correct Answer:** B) commensurate
