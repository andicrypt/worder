**Question:** 

Which of the following individuals would most likely be described as a zealot?

A) A scientist who cautiously analyzes data before drawing conclusions.

B) A politician who is known for frequently compromising to achieve bipartisan support.

C) A protester who passionately advocates for environmental conservation, refusing to consider any opposing views.

D) A teacher who encourages students to explore multiple perspectives on historical events.

**Answer:** 

C) A protester who passionately advocates for environmental conservation, refusing to consider any opposing views.
