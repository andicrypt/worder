**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best demonstrates the meaning of the word "forestall"?

A) The manager decided to forestall any potential misunderstandings by clearly outlining the project goals at the beginning of the meeting.

B) The chef added extra spices to the dish to enhance the flavor and make it more appealing to the guests.

C) The artist's work was known for its vibrant colors and unique textures that captivated audiences.

D) The athlete trained vigorously every day to improve his performance for the upcoming championship.

**Correct Answer:**

A) The manager decided to forestall any potential misunderstandings by clearly outlining the project goals at the beginning of the meeting.
