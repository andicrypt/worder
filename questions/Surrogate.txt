Choose the word that most closely matches the meaning of "surrogate."

A) Precursor  
B) Proxy  
C) Opponent  
D) Benefactor  

**Correct Answer: B) Proxy**

Explanation: "Surrogate" means a substitute, especially a person deputizing for another in a specific role or office. "Proxy" is a synonym as it also refers to someone who acts on behalf of another.
