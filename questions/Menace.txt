**Question:**

In the context of environmental preservation, climate change can be considered a significant _______ to global ecosystems, as it poses a threat to biodiversity and the stability of various habitats.

A) boon  
B) menace  
C) ally  
D) distraction  

**Correct Answer: B) menace**

**Question:**  

In the context of environmental conservation, many experts believe that climate change is the greatest ______ to biodiversity, as it threatens the survival of countless species.

A) Benefit  
B) Menace  
C) Advantage  
D) Aid  

**Correct Answer:** B) Menace
