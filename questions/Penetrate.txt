Question: In the context of understanding complex ideas, which of the following sentences correctly uses the word "penetrate"?

A) The sunlight was able to penetrate the dense forest, illuminating the ground beneath.

B) The professor's detailed explanation finally allowed the concept to penetrate the students' minds. 

C) The athlete trained hard to penetrate the opposing team's defense.

D) The company managed to penetrate the market with its new line of products.

Correct Answer: B) The professor's detailed explanation finally allowed the concept to penetrate the students' minds.
