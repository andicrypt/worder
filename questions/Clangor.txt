Certainly! Here is a GRE-style vocabulary question for the word "clangor":

---

**Vocabulary Question:**

The clangor of the city streets was so overwhelming that Jenny found it difficult to hold a conversation with her friend as they walked.

In the context of the sentence above, "clangor" most nearly means:

A) **A continuous loud banging or ringing sound**

B) A gentle and soothing melody

C) A brief and sudden silence

D) A soft and distant whisper

---

**Correct Answer:** A) A continuous loud banging or ringing sound
