**Question:**

In the context of environmental science, individuals who are highly *susceptible* to air pollution are often advised to stay indoors on days when air quality is poor. What does the word *susceptible* most nearly mean in this sentence?

**Options:**

A) Immune

B) Resistant

C) Vulnerable

D) Unconcerned

**Correct Answer:** C) Vulnerable

**Question:**

In the context of health and wellness, individuals who do not receive vaccinations may be more _______ to infectious diseases, as they lack the protective antibodies that immunizations provide.

A) immune  
B) susceptible  
C) indifferent  
D) resilient  

**Correct Answer:** B) susceptible
