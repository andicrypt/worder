**Question:**  

In an unexpected twist, the fire station burned down, an incident most people found quite ______.

A) Fortuitous  
B) Ironic  
C) Predictable  
D) Auspicious  

**Correct Answer:** B) Ironic

**Question:**

In a surprising twist of events, the fire station burned down due to faulty wiring. This situation can best be described as:

A) Tragic  
B) Ironic  
C) Predictable  
D) Mundane  

**Correct Answer: B) Ironic**
