Question: In recent years, the artist's work has been described as increasingly ***fringe***, characterized by its departure from traditional techniques and subjects.

Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "fringe" as used in the sentence above?

A) Conventional  
B) Mainstream  
C) Peripheral  
D) Central  

Correct Answer: C) Peripheral

Question:

The scientist's theories were considered ______, as they deviated significantly from established scientific norms and were often met with skepticism by the mainstream academic community.

A) conventional  
B) fringe  
C) orthodox  
D) central  

Correct Answer: B) fringe
