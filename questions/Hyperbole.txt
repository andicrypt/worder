Question: In the context of creative writing, which of the following sentences best demonstrates the use of hyperbole?

A) The sun was setting slowly, painting the sky in shades of orange and pink.

B) I have a million things to do today, and I don't know where to start. 

C) She whispered softly so as not to disturb the sleeping baby.

D) The scientist presented his findings with precise and detailed explanations.

Correct Answer: B) I have a million things to do today, and I don't know where to start. 

Explanation: Option B uses hyperbole by exaggerating the number of tasks ("a million things") to emphasize being overwhelmed, without the statement being meant literally.
