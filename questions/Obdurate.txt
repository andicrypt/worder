**Question:**  

Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "obdurate"?  

A) Flexible  
B) Yielding  
C) Stubborn  
D) Compliant  

**Correct Answer:** C) Stubborn

**Question:**

In the face of overwhelming evidence against his plan, the CEO remained ____, insisting that his approach was the only viable option.

A) Flexible  
B) Obdurate  
C) Compromising  
D) Yielding  

**Correct Answer:** B) Obdurate
