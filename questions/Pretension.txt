**Question:**

In the context of art and social gatherings, the term "pretension" most likely refers to:

A) A genuine and humble display of talent

B) An attempt to impress others by pretending to be more important or cultured than one really is

C) A lack of interest in social status or material wealth

D) A sincere and straightforward interaction with others

**Correct Answer:** B) An attempt to impress others by pretending to be more important or cultured than one really is
