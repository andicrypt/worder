Certainly! Here is a GRE-style vocabulary question for the word "scrutinize":

---

**Question:**  

In her role as a quality control specialist, Maria was required to __________ each product before it was approved for shipment.

A) overlook  
B) scrutinize  
C) ignore  
D) neglect  

**Correct Answer:** B) scrutinize

**Explanation:** The word "scrutinize" means to examine or inspect closely and thoroughly, which is what Maria needs to do as a quality control specialist to ensure each product meets the necessary standards before shipment.
