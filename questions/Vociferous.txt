**Question:**

In the town hall meeting, the vociferous advocate for environmental reforms made her opinions known in a manner that was both _______ and unyielding, capturing the attention of everyone present.

**Options:**

A) Subdued  
B) Muffled  
C) Strident  
D) Modest  

**Correct Answer: C) Strident**  

**Explanation:** The word "vociferous" describes someone who is loud and forceful in expressing their opinions. "Strident" is similar in meaning, indicating a loud and harsh or forceful manner. The other options are antonyms or unrelated in meaning.
