**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best demonstrates the meaning of the word "confound"?

A) The magician's performance was so predictable that it failed to confound the audience.  
B) The new teacher's methods were so innovative that they confounded the students.  
C) The instructions were so clear that they confounded everyone who read them.  
D) The routine was practiced so many times that it no longer confounded the dancers.  

**Answer:**

B) The new teacher's methods were so innovative that they confounded the students.
