Question: 

Which of the following best describes the term "foresight"?

A) Apathy

B) Intuition

C) Retrospection

D) **Prediction**

Explanation: 

"Foresight" refers to the ability to predict or the action of predicting what will happen or be needed in the future. Therefore, the correct answer is D) Prediction, as it aligns with the definition. The other options do not accurately capture the essence of foresight.
