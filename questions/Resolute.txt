Question:
In the face of adversity, the ____________ leader inspired her team to persevere and overcome obstacles with determination and unwavering resolve.

A) tentative
B) resolute
C) vacillating
D) ambivalent

Correct Answer: B) resolute

**Question:**  

Which of the following best describes someone who is *resolute*?

A) Indecisive and wavering in their actions  
B) Admirably purposeful and determined  
C) Easily discouraged by setbacks  
D) Flexible and open to changing plans  

**Answer:**  
B) Admirably purposeful and determined
