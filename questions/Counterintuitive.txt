**Question:**

Which of the following situations best exemplifies something that is counterintuitive?

A) A doctor prescribing antibiotics for a bacterial infection.

B) A teacher using interactive games to make learning more engaging.

C) A financial expert advising clients to invest in stocks during an economic downturn, which ultimately leads to significant profits.

D) A chef using fresh ingredients to enhance the flavor of a dish.

**Answer:**

C) A financial expert advising clients to invest in stocks during an economic downturn, which ultimately leads to significant profits. (Correct Answer)
