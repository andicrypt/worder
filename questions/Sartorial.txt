**Question:**

Which of the following sentences uses the word "sartorial" correctly?

A) The chef's sartorial skills were evident in the way she prepared the gourmet meal.

B) His sartorial choices always included bold ties and perfectly fitted suits. 

C) She showed her sartorial talents by playing the piano beautifully at the concert.

D) The artist's sartorial creations were displayed in the gallery for everyone to admire.

**Correct Answer: B) His sartorial choices always included bold ties and perfectly fitted suits.**
