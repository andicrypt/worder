**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the concept of "serendipity"?

A) A scientist conducts a series of controlled experiments and confirms a long-standing hypothesis.

B) An author meticulously plans a novel over several years and finally publishes it to critical acclaim.

C) A traveler accidentally takes a wrong turn and stumbles upon a charming village festival, leading to unexpected friendships and unforgettable experiences.

D) A student spends months studying diligently for an exam and achieves the top score in the class.

**Correct Answer: C) A traveler accidentally takes a wrong turn and stumbles upon a charming village festival, leading to unexpected friendships and unforgettable experiences.**
