**Question:**

The sudden changes in the company's leadership led to a sense of disquiet among the employees, causing them to feel _____ about their job security.

A) tranquility

B) confidence

C) uneasiness

D) enthusiasm

**Correct Answer:** C) uneasiness

**Question:**   
Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "disquiet"?  

A) Tranquility  
B) Contentment  
C) Agitation  
D) Serenity  

**Correct Answer:**  
C) Agitation
