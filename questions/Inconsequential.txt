**Question:**

In the context of a major life decision, such as choosing a career path, which of the following would most likely be considered inconsequential?

A) The benefits and perks associated with the job

B) The location of the job and its impact on your daily commute

C) The color of the pen you use to sign your employment contract

D) The potential for career advancement within the company

**Correct Answer: C) The color of the pen you use to sign your employment contract**

Explanation: In the context of choosing a career path, the color of the pen is something that would have no significant effect or result on the decision, making it inconsequential.
