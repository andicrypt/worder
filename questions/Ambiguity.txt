**Question:**

In the context of literary analysis, the term "ambiguity" most closely refers to:

A) A clear and precise statement that leaves no room for interpretation.

B) A narrative technique that deliberately leaves certain elements open to multiple interpretations.

C) A strict adherence to traditional storytelling structures and conventions.

D) A method of writing that focuses on factual accuracy and unambiguous language.

**Correct Answer:**

B) A narrative technique that deliberately leaves certain elements open to multiple interpretations.
