Which of the following scenarios best exemplifies a quandary?

A) A tourist easily finds their way around a city using a detailed map.

B) A student is uncertain about which college to attend, as both have appealing programs and scholarships.

C) A chef confidently prepares a dish they have mastered over years of practice.

D) An athlete follows a well-established training routine without any disruptions.

**Correct Answer: B) A student is uncertain about which college to attend, as both have appealing programs and scholarships.**

Explanation: A quandary refers to a state of perplexity or uncertainty over what to do in a difficult situation, which is exemplified in option B where the student is unsure of which college to choose due to equally appealing options.
