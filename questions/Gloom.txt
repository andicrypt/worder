**Question:**

In the passage, the protagonist walks through the abandoned town with a sense of deep gloom, unable to shake off the feeling of hopelessness that has settled over him. Which of the following words best captures the meaning of "gloom" as used in the passage?

A) Elation  
B) Despondency  
C) Jubilation  
D) Serenity  

**Correct Answer: B) Despondency**

**Question:**

In the aftermath of the unexpected defeat, the atmosphere in the locker room was filled with a palpable sense of ____; the players sat in silence, their heads bowed and eyes downcast.

A) Euphoria  
B) Gloom  
C) Elation  
D) Jubilation  

**Correct Answer:** B) Gloom
