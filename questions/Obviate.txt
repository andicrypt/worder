**Question:**

In efforts to improve the safety of the new bridge, engineers implemented numerous design features to _____ potential structural failures.

A) obviate  
B) exacerbate  
C) expedite  
D) obfuscate  

**Correct Answer: A) obviate**  

**Explanation:**  
The word "obviate" means to avoid or prevent. In the context of the sentence, the engineers are taking measures to prevent potential structural failures, which aligns with the meaning of "obviate."
