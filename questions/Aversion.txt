**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the meaning of the word "aversion"?

A) Maria has an aversion to loud music, so she often attends rock concerts.

B) Despite her aversion to seafood, Emma eagerly ordered the shrimp cocktail at dinner.

C) Tom's aversion to smoking led him to campaign for stricter tobacco regulations. 

D) Lisa's aversion to public speaking inspires her to participate in every debate competition.

**Answer:**

C) Tom's aversion to smoking led him to campaign for stricter tobacco regulations.
