Which of the following words most closely matches the meaning of "panacea"?

A) Enigma  
B) Elixir  
C) Conundrum  
D) Quandary  

**Answer: B) Elixir**  

Explanation: "Panacea" refers to a remedy or solution for all diseases or difficulties, similar to the word "elixir," which can also imply a magical or universal cure. The other options do not convey the same idea.

Question: In a world constantly seeking solutions to complex problems, many people yearn for a panacea, which is best described as a:

A) temporary relief from pain  
B) detailed analysis of a situation  
C) remedy for all difficulties  
D) method of avoiding hard work  

Correct Answer: C) remedy for all difficulties
