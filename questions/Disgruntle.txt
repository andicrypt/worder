**Question:**  

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the meaning of the word "disgruntle"?  

A) A student is delighted to receive an unexpected scholarship for her outstanding academic performance.  
B) A manager recognizes an employee's hard work and awards him the Employee of the Month title.  
C) A customer waits for over an hour for their meal to be served, only to find it cold and undercooked, leaving them quite annoyed.  
D) A family enjoys a pleasant and relaxing day at the beach, with perfect weather and calm waters.  

**Correct Answer:** C) A customer waits for over an hour for their meal to be served, only to find it cold and undercooked, leaving them quite annoyed.
