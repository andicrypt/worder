**Question:**  
In an effort to increase productivity and foster innovation, the company decided to remove the outdated policies that seemed to _____ the creativity of its employees.

A) Enhance  
B) Manacle  
C) Foster  
D) Liberate  

**Correct Answer:** B) Manacle

**Question:**

In a strict corporate environment, excessive bureaucracy can often ________ the creativity and innovation of employees, preventing them from expressing their full potential.

A) Enhance

B) Manacle

C) Foster

D) Liberate

**Correct Answer: B) Manacle**
