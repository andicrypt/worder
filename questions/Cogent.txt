**Question:**

Which of the following statements best exemplifies a cogent argument?

A) The presentation was filled with irrelevant anecdotes, leaving the audience confused about the main point.

B) The speaker's argument was based on solid evidence, clearly presented, and left little room for doubt. 

C) The lecturer's ideas were scattered and lacked coherence, making it difficult to follow.

D) The report was lengthy and verbose, with numerous tangential points that obscured the main argument.

**Correct Answer: B) The speaker's argument was based on solid evidence, clearly presented, and left little room for doubt.**
