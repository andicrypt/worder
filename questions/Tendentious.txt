**Question:**

In the debate, the speaker's arguments were criticized for being overly tendentious, as they clearly aimed to __________.

A) provide a balanced analysis  
B) promote a specific agenda  
C) confuse the audience  
D) entertain the listeners  

**Correct Answer: B) promote a specific agenda**

Choose the word that most closely matches the meaning of "tendentious."

A) Objective  
B) Impartial  
C) Biased  
D) Unbiased  

Correct Answer: C) Biased
