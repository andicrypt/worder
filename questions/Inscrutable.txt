**Question:**

The professor's lecture was so **inscrutable** that most of the students left the class feeling more confused than when they entered. In this context, "inscrutable" most nearly means:

A) Transparent  
B) Mysterious  
C) Obvious  
D) Predictable  

**Correct Answer:** B) Mysterious

**Question:**  

The ancient manuscript was considered **inscrutable** by many scholars, as its symbols and language were unlike anything they had encountered before. Which of the following words best matches the meaning of "inscrutable"?  

A) Transparent  
B) Obvious  
C) Mysterious  
D) Predictable  

**Correct Answer:** C) Mysterious
