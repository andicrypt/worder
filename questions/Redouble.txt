Certainly! Here is a GRE-style vocabulary question for the word "redouble":

---

In an effort to manage the crisis, the company decided to _____ its efforts to improve customer satisfaction, implementing a series of new policies and initiatives.

A) diminish  
B) redouble  
C) abandon  
D) trivialize  

**Correct Answer: B) redouble**

---

In this question, the context suggests an increase in efforts, which aligns with the definition of "redouble" as making something more intense or greater.
