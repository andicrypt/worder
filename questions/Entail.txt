**Question:**

Which of the following sentences uses the word "entail" correctly?

A) The new policy will entail a decrease in overall productivity without any additional resources.

B) She was excited to entail her favorite book to her friend as a gift.

C) The chef decided to entail the recipe with extra spices for more flavor.

D) His job entails him to travel to different countries for leisure.

**Correct Answer:**

A) The new policy will entail a decrease in overall productivity without any additional resources.
