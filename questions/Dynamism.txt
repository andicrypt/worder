**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the concept of "dynamism"?

A) A library where the books have remained untouched and unchanged for decades.

B) A bustling startup company rapidly expanding its product line and workforce. 

C) A sleepy town where nothing much has changed over the last fifty years.

D) A museum displaying ancient artifacts with strict preservation rules.

**Correct Answer: B) A bustling startup company rapidly expanding its product line and workforce.**
