**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best uses the word "meticulous"?

A) She was known for her meticulous approach to organizing events, ensuring that every detail was perfect.  
B) His speech was filled with vague generalities, lacking any meticulous thought or preparation.  
C) The artist was renowned for his spontaneous and haphazard style, never relying on any meticulous planning.  
D) Although he was in a hurry, he decided to be meticulous and completed the task with only a cursory glance.  

**Correct Answer:** A) She was known for her meticulous approach to organizing events, ensuring that every detail was perfect.
