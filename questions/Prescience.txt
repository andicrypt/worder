**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best demonstrates the meaning of "prescience"?

A) The chef's prescience about the popularity of fusion cuisine led him to open a restaurant that combined several culinary traditions.

B) The author's prescience in writing complex characters made the novel difficult to understand for most readers.

C) The engineer's prescience in designing the bridge was evident in its ornate and elaborate decorations.

D) The athlete's prescience about the importance of teamwork was seen in his insistence on practicing alone.

**Correct Answer:**

A) The chef's prescience about the popularity of fusion cuisine led him to open a restaurant that combined several culinary traditions.
