Question:
The security guard's **vigilance** prevented any unauthorized individuals from entering the restricted area.

Which of the following words is the closest in meaning to "vigilance"?

A) Complacency
B) Diligence
C) Indifference
D) Ineptitude

Correct Answer: B) Diligence

Question:
Which of the following best defines the word "vigilance"?

A) A state of deep thought and contemplation
B) The act of being overly cautious and paranoid
C) The action or state of keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties
D) A feeling of contentment and satisfaction

Correct Answer: C) The action or state of keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties
