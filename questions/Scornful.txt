Question: Which of the following sentences best uses the word "scornful"?

A) The artist was scornful of critics who failed to recognize the depth of his work.  
B) She was careful and scornful in her approach to solving the complex puzzle.  
C) He was scornful and shy when meeting new people at social gatherings.  
D) The scornful manager praised the team for their exceptional performance.  

Correct Answer: A) The artist was scornful of critics who failed to recognize the depth of his work.
