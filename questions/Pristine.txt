Question: 

In the context of environmental conservation, the term "pristine" is most likely used to describe which of the following?

A) A forest that has been extensively logged and is in need of reforestation.  
B) A river that flows through a densely populated city, carrying industrial waste.  
C) A beach that has been preserved in its natural state, untouched by human development.  
D) A mountain range that is popular for skiing and has numerous resorts and lodges.  

Correct Answer: C) A beach that has been preserved in its natural state, untouched by human development.
