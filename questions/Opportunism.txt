**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the concept of "opportunism"?

A) A company that develops a long-term strategic plan and follows it diligently to achieve its goals.

B) An entrepreneur who seizes a sudden market trend to launch a new product, without considering the ethical implications or long-term effects. 

C) A student who carefully plans their study schedule weeks in advance to prepare for final exams.

D) A government that implements a well-thought-out policy after extensive research and consultation with experts.

**Correct Answer: B) An entrepreneur who seizes a sudden market trend to launch a new product, without considering the ethical implications or long-term effects.**
