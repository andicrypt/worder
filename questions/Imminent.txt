**Question:**

The dark clouds gathered in the sky, signaling that a storm was __________.

A) inconceivable  
B) imminent  
C) indefinite  
D) negligible  

**Correct Answer: B) imminent**

**Question:**

The meteorologists warned that a severe thunderstorm was __________, urging residents to take shelter immediately.

A) Imminent  
B) Retrospective  
C) Tranquil  
D) Prolonged  

**Answer:**

A) Imminent
