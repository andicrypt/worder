**Question:**

In recent studies, the new drug was praised for its high level of efficacy in treating the disease, leading researchers to conclude that it was significantly more ______ than previous treatments.

A) ambiguous  
B) ineffective  
C) potent  
D) costly  

**Correct Answer: C) potent**  

Explanation: The word "efficacy" refers to the ability of something to produce desired results or its effectiveness. In the context of the sentence, a drug with high efficacy would be more potent or effective than previous treatments.
