**Question:**  
Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "innocuous"?

A) Malicious  
B) Harmless  
C) Pernicious  
D) Deleterious  

**Answer:**  
B) Harmless

**Question:**

Which of the following sentences uses the word "innocuous" correctly?

A) The venomous snake's bite was anything but innocuous, causing severe symptoms almost immediately.

B) Despite its fierce appearance, the small garden snake was entirely innocuous, posing no threat to humans. 

C) The judge's verdict was innocuous, resulting in a lengthy prison sentence for the defendant.

D) Her criticism was innocuous, leaving him feeling deeply hurt and offended.

**Correct Answer:**

B) Despite its fierce appearance, the small garden snake was entirely innocuous, posing no threat to humans.
