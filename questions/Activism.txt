**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the concept of "activism"?

A) A company reduces its workforce to cut costs and increase profits.

B) A group of volunteers organizes a marathon to raise funds for a local orphanage.

C) A community gathers to celebrate a cultural festival with traditional music and food.

D) A coalition of environmentalists launches a series of protests to advocate for stricter pollution controls.

**Correct Answer: D) A coalition of environmentalists launches a series of protests to advocate for stricter pollution controls.**
