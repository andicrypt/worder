**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the concept of "extravagance"?

A) Saving up money over several months to purchase a necessary appliance.

B) Carefully budgeting expenses to ensure all bills are paid on time.

C) Spending a significant amount of money on a lavish vacation, despite being in debt. *Correct Answer*

D) Donating a small portion of one's income to a local charity. 

**Explanation:**

The correct answer is C) Spending a significant amount of money on a lavish vacation, despite being in debt. This scenario illustrates the concept of extravagance, which involves spending excessively or imprudently, especially in a manner that exceeds one's financial means.
