Which of the following best describes the term "empathy"?

A) The ability to remain detached and indifferent to the emotions of others.

B) The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.

C) The tendency to dominate conversations and impose one's views on others.

D) The skill of accurately predicting future events based on current trends.

**Correct Answer: B) The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.**
