**Question:**

In her speech, the CEO chose to foreground the company's commitment to sustainability, ensuring that this aspect would be the most prominent feature of the presentation. Which of the following actions best illustrates the concept of "foregrounding"?

A) Minimizing the importance of customer feedback in decision-making processes.

B) Highlighting the new product line in the marketing campaign to attract more customers.

C) Ignoring the recent achievements of the research team during the annual meeting.

D) Concealing the financial challenges faced by the company in the quarterly report.

**Correct Answer:**

B) Highlighting the new product line in the marketing campaign to attract more customers.
