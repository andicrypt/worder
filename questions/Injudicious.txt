**Question:**

Which of the following sentences uses the word "injudicious" correctly?

A) Her injudicious planning led to a perfectly executed event that everyone enjoyed.

B) The injudicious decision to invest all his savings in a single stock resulted in significant financial loss. 

C) The injudicious nature of the experiment was evident when it yielded accurate and reliable results.

D) His injudicious generosity earned him the reputation of being a shrewd and calculating businessman.

**Answer:**

B) The injudicious decision to invest all his savings in a single stock resulted in significant financial loss.
