**Question:**

In the face of criticism, the politician delivered a brazen speech that left the audience both shocked and impressed by his unwavering confidence. Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "brazen"?

A) Timid  
B) Modest  
C) Shameless  
D) Hesitant  

**Correct Answer: C) Shameless**  

**Explanation:**  
The word "brazen" means to be bold and without shame, especially in situations that might typically cause embarrassment or require discretion. "Shameless" is the most similar in meaning, as it implies a lack of shame or restraint.
