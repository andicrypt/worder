**Question:**  

Which of the following sentences best demonstrates the meaning of the word "malign"?

A) The teacher praised the student for her excellent performance on the exam.  
B) The journalist wrote an article to malign the politician by spreading false rumors about his personal life.  
C) The doctor advised the patient to maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly.  
D) The actor received an award for his outstanding performance in the film.  

**Correct Answer:** B) The journalist wrote an article to malign the politician by spreading false rumors about his personal life.
