**Question:**  

Which of the following sentences uses the word "insightful" correctly?  

A) The novel was so insightful that it left many readers confused about its meaning.  

B) The insightful documentary provided a deep understanding of the complex issue, leaving viewers with a new perspective.  

C) His insightful comments were so vague that no one could understand his point.  

D) The insightful painting was so abstract that it did not convey any clear message.  

**Correct Answer:** B) The insightful documentary provided a deep understanding of the complex issue, leaving viewers with a new perspective.
