**Question:**

In the context of social movements, which of the following best illustrates the concept of "apathy"?

A) A community rallying together to support a local cause.

B) A neighborhood organizing a clean-up event to beautify their area.

C) A person choosing to ignore political debates and not voting in elections.

D) A group of students forming a club to promote environmental awareness.

**Correct Answer:**

C) A person choosing to ignore political debates and not voting in elections.
