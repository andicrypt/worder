**Question:**

In the context of evaluating the credibility of a source, if a researcher describes the findings as "dubious," what is the most likely implication of this term?

**A.** The findings are groundbreaking and innovative.  
**B.** The findings are irrelevant and outdated.  
**C.** The findings are suspicious and questionable.  
**D.** The findings are conclusive and definitive.

**Correct Answer: C.** The findings are suspicious and questionable.
