Question:
The student was quick to demur when the teacher assigned a challenging project, expressing concern about the tight deadline. Which of the following is the closest in meaning to "demur"?

A) Agree
B) Disagree
C) Hesitate
D) Comply

Correct answer: C) Hesitate

Question:
In the meeting, Sarah was the only one to **demur** when the team proposed a drastic change to the project timeline, expressing her concerns about the feasibility of such a tight deadline.

Which of the following words is most similar in meaning to "demur" as used in the sentence above?

A) Accept
B) Approve
C) Challenge
D) Comply

Correct Answer: C) Challenge
