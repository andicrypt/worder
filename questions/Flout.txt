Question:
In the novel, the protagonist was known to __________ authority figures and often broke the rules without any regard for consequences.

A) conform
B) adhere to
C) flout
D) comply with

Correct answer: C) flout

**Question:**  
In a blatant act of rebellion, the students chose to _____ the school's strict dress code by wearing casual attire to the formal assembly.

A) adhere to  
B) flout  
C) enforce  
D) comprehend  

**Correct Answer:** B) flout
