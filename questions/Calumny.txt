**Question:**

Which of the following sentences best demonstrates the meaning of the word "calumny"?

A) The scientist aimed to **replicate** the experiment to verify the results.

B) The politician's career was nearly ruined by the **calumny** spread by his opponents.

C) The teacher encouraged a **synergy** between students to foster collaborative learning.

D) The artist's work was praised for its **aesthetic** appeal and innovative techniques.

**Correct Answer: B**

---

The sentence in option B correctly illustrates the meaning of "calumny" by showing how false and slanderous statements can harm someone's reputation.
