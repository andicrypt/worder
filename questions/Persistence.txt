**Question:**

Which of the following best illustrates the concept of "persistence"?

A) Abandoning a project after encountering the first obstacle.

B) Continuing to practice piano every day despite struggling to master a difficult piece.

C) Quickly adapting to a new situation with little difficulty.

D) Skipping a workout session because of a minor inconvenience.

**Correct Answer:**

B) Continuing to practice piano every day despite struggling to master a difficult piece.
