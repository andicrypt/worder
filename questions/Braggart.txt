**Question:**

Which of the following individuals would most likely be described as a "braggart"?

A) A scientist who publishes research quietly and shares credit with colleagues.

B) A volunteer who modestly donates time to community service without seeking recognition.

C) A traveler who constantly talks about their luxurious vacations and expensive possessions.

D) A teacher who encourages students to achieve their best without mentioning their own accolades.

**Correct Answer: C) A traveler who constantly talks about their luxurious vacations and expensive possessions.**
