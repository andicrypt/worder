**Question:**

Which of the following scenarios best illustrates the concept of "narcissism"?

A) Maria spends her weekends volunteering at the local animal shelter and enjoys helping others in need.

B) Jason constantly checks his reflection in every mirror he passes and frequently boasts about his achievements on social media.

C) Emily prefers reading books over watching television, as she finds it more intellectually stimulating.

D) Robert is dedicated to improving his physical fitness and follows a strict training regimen to prepare for a marathon.

**Correct Answer: B)** Jason constantly checks his reflection in every mirror he passes and frequently boasts about his achievements on social media.
