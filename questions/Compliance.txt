**Question:**  

In the context of a corporate setting, a manager who is known for his compliance is likely to be viewed as someone who:  

A) Challenges decisions and often defies authority.  
B) Consistently agrees with superiors without scrutiny.  
C) Innovates and introduces new ideas to the team.  
D) Demonstrates a strong sense of independence and self-reliance.  

**Correct Answer:** B) Consistently agrees with superiors without scrutiny.
