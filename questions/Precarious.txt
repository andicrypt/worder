**Question:**

In the context of the following sentence, what does the word "precarious" most nearly mean?

"The climbers found themselves in a precarious position on the narrow ledge, where one wrong move could lead to disaster."

A) Stable  
B) Secure  
C) Uncertain  
D) Predictable  

**Answer:**

C) Uncertain

**Question:**

In the context of the passage, the term "precarious" most closely means:

A) Secure  
B) Certain  
C) Unstable  
D) Predictable
