**Question:**

In the context of a formal dinner party, which of the following behaviors would most likely be described as unseemly?

A) Engaging in polite conversation with fellow guests

B) Wearing a formal evening gown

C) Interrupting the host with loud jokes and laughter

D) Complimenting the chef on the exquisite meal

**Correct Answer: C) Interrupting the host with loud jokes and laughter**
