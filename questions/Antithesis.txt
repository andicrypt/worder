**Question:**

In literature, the concept of the hero and the villain often serve as the __________, highlighting the stark difference between good and evil.

A) Synthesis

B) Antithesis

C) Analogy

D) Homonym

**Correct Answer: B) Antithesis**

**Explanation:** 

The word "antithesis" refers to a contrast or opposition between two things. In this context, the hero and the villain are juxtaposed to emphasize the opposition between good and evil.
