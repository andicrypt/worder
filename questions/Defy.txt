Certainly! Here is a GRE-style vocabulary question for the word "Defy":

---

**Question:**

In the context of the rebellious teenager's actions, which of the following best describes his attitude towards his parents' rules?

A) Conform

B) Defy

C) Comply

D) Obey

**Answer:**  
B) Defy

---

Explanation: The word "defy" means to resist or challenge openly and act against the wishes or decrees of someone. In the context of the rebellious teenager, "defy" accurately describes his attitude towards his parents' rules. The other options (Conform, Comply, Obey) imply agreement or submission, which do not fit the context of resistance or challenge.
