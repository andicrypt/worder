Certainly! Here is a GRE-style vocabulary question for the word "supersede":

---

In the fast-paced world of technology, new innovations often _________ older models, making them obsolete.

A) undermine  
B) supersede  
C) replicate  
D) preserve  

**Correct Answer: B) supersede**

---

Explanation: The context of the sentence suggests that new innovations replace or take the place of older models, which aligns with the definition of "supersede."
