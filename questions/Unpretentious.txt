**Question:**

Which of the following words is most nearly opposite in meaning to "unpretentious"?

A) Modest

B) Ostentatious

C) Humble

D) Simple

**Correct Answer: B) Ostentatious**

**Explanation:**  
"Unpretentious" describes someone who does not try to impress others by pretending to be more important or talented than they are. The opposite would be someone who does try to impress others, often through showy or extravagant means. "Ostentatious" fits this description, as it means characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice.
