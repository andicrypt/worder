**Question:**

In an effort to make the classic novel more accessible to young readers, the editor decided to _______ the text, ensuring that the essential narrative remained intact while reducing its length.

A) Elongate  
B) Abridge  
C) Obfuscate  
D) Amplify  

**Correct Answer: B) Abridge**  

**Explanation:**  
The word "abridge" means to shorten a piece of writing without losing the sense. The context of the sentence suggests that the text was shortened while preserving its essential narrative, which aligns with the definition of "abridge."
