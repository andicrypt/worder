Question:

In the face of _________ evidence of environmental degradation, governments worldwide are being urged to take immediate action.

A) diminishing  
B) mounting  
C) trivial  
D) negligible  

Correct Answer: B) mounting

Question: In the context of increasing concerns over climate change, the evidence of environmental impact is __________, leading to a greater urgency for international cooperation.

A) Diminishing  
B) Stabilizing  
C) Mounting  
D) Negligible  

Correct Answer: C) Mounting
