**Question:**

In the context of describing a garden filled with rare plants, the word "exotic" most nearly means:

A) Ordinary  
B) Dull  
C) Unfamiliar  
D) Transparent  

**Correct Answer: C) Unfamiliar**

**Explanation:**  
The word "exotic" is used to describe something that is attractive or striking because it is colorful or out of the ordinary, which often means it is unfamiliar or not commonly seen. Therefore, the option that best fits this description is "Unfamiliar."
